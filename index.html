Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between basic chatbot and AI customer support?

Basic chatbots follow predetermined rules and can only respond to specific commands. Our AI customer support uses machine learning to understand natural language, context, and can handle complex, unpredictable conversations while continuously learning from interactions to improve performance.

How long does website development typically take?

Basic websites are typically delivered within 10-14 days. Business websites with more complex functionality take 2-3 weeks. Enterprise solutions vary based on requirements but we provide detailed timelines during consultation. We pride ourselves on meeting deadlines without compromising quality.

Do you provide ongoing support after development?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages. Basic websites include 1 month of support, business websites include 3 months, and we offer ongoing support contracts for all enterprise clients. Our support includes technical assistance, minor updates, and performance monitoring.

Can I upgrade my plan as my business grows?

Absolutely! We've designed our services to scale with your business. You can easily upgrade your website, hosting, or AI support at any time. We prorate upgrades and ensure seamless transitions without service interruption.

What makes your hosting optimized for African businesses?

Our hosting infrastructure includes servers located in Africa for faster loading times for local visitors, specialized CDN configurations for African networks, support for local payment integrations, and technical support staff familiar with African internet infrastructure and challenges.
