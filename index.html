Frequently Asked Questions

How does AfrixaAI handle African languages and dialects?

Our AI is specially trained on African languages, accents, and cultural contexts. We support all major African languages including Swahili, Hausa, Yoruba, Zulu, Amharic, and Arabic, with continuous learning to understand local dialects and expressions.

Can I integrate with my existing systems?

Absolutely! AfrixaAI integrates seamlessly with popular CRM systems, WhatsApp Business, Facebook Messenger, and custom solutions through our API. Our technical team will ensure smooth integration with your existing tools.

What makes AfrixaAI different from other chatbots?

Unlike generic solutions, AfrixaAI is built specifically for African businesses. We understand local contexts, languages, and customer behavior patterns. Our AI provides culturally relevant responses and handles African payment systems seamlessly.

How quickly can I get started?

Most customers are up and running within 24 hours! Our streamlined onboarding process requires minimal technical expertise. We handle the setup while you focus on your business.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 customer support with local African numbers, dedicated account managers for enterprise clients, and comprehensive documentation. Our support team understands African business environments and can help in multiple languages.
